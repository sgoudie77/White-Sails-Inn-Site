White Sails Inn

The White Sails Inn is located on the Eastport Peninsula, 15 minutes drive off of the Trans Canada Highway.

Our Beach House and Cottages are directly across from Eastport Beach with direct access to the High Tide Trail.

Please call us to book your reservation and we’ll be happy to help you plan your best stay in Newfoundland and Labrador’s Central Region.
